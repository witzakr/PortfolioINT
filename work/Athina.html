    Athina Portfolio

    The Athina portfolio project was my first client project, which I worked on during my second semester. The objective was to create a portfolio website for the client, and we followed the waterfall method, breaking the project into research, design, development, and testing phases. My role was primarily as a developer, and I was responsible for the Home and Contact pages. I was also heavily involved in the design phase, particularly in creating prototypes using Figma.
    This project was a great learning experience for me as it was my first time working with a client. It taught me important skills such as effective communication with clients, delivering work on time, and presenting the final product.
