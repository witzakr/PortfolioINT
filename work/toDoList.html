    To Do List

    In the second semester, I created a simple To-Do list assignment to demonstrate basic JavaScript skills. The list allows users to add activities, modify their status, and remove them. It also includes functionality for filtering and displaying the items based on their status, such as pending or completed tasks. This project served as a practical exercise in working with JavaScript to manage dynamic content.
