    Counter Challenge

    The Counter Challenge was an assignment that tasked me with creating a website featuring a countdown timer. The primary requirements included ensuring the timer counted down accurately while also dynamically changing colors along with the background. One of the main challenges was to maintain readability and visibility amidst these dynamic changes. Additionally, I incorporated APIs into the project to translate the "hello world" text into ten different languages, adding an extra layer of complexity and functionality.
