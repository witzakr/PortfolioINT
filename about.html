    About me

    Hello! I'm Wit Zakrzewski, a 21-year-old student at Fontys University of Applied Sciences in Eindhoven, specializing in Media Design. I'm passionate about bridging the worlds of technology and art, creating innovative solutions in the dynamic field of ICT-media design.
    Outside my studies, I enjoy drawing, particularly experimenting with simple shading techniques. I'm also an avid reader, especially when it comes to history books. Sports are a big part of my life, with basketball being my favorite.
    In addition to my creative interests, I have coding experience in Python, C#, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, blending technology with my artistic inclinations. My current goal is to complete my course, and after that, I plan to pursue a master's degree in computer science, with the ambition of becoming a professional Full-stack developer.
    Join me on this exciting academic journey!
